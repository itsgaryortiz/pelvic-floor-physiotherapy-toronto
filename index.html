What is Pelvic Floor Physiotherapy?

Pelvic floor physiotherapy is a specialized treatment that focuses on strengthening and rehabilitating the muscles of the pelvic floor. These muscles support the bladder, uterus, and rectum, and dysfunction can lead to problems such as incontinence, pelvic pain, and sexual dysfunction.

Conditions Treated with Pelvic Floor Physiotherapy

  • Urinary Incontinence: We can help treat both stress and urge incontinence with tailored exercises and techniques.
  • Pelvic Pain: If you're experiencing pain during sex, menstruation, or physical activity, pelvic floor therapy can provide relief.
  • Postpartum Recovery: After childbirth, pelvic floor physiotherapy can help with recovery and prevent long-term complications.
  • Prolapse: Pelvic floor therapy can help manage symptoms of pelvic organ prolapse, improving quality of life.
